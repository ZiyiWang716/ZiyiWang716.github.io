EMERGENCY CALL

    Tim Berners-Lee, the inventor of Word Wide Web, talked about his opinion about our contemporary web life in his article Long Live The Web. And yes, Web has made our life so different from the past by spreading information universally to people around the world.

    However, with its huge development within just about two decades, Web is becoming more and more like a double-edged sword. While its powerfulness has greatly benefited each of us, it is also at the same time being malevolently used by a group of Web users who only consider their own interests. Here, I list a few crises our Web is facing nowadays that are most common. Hope we all can make an effort to eliminate these dangers so that we can have a better wordwide virtual community in the near future!

Long Live the Web!!!!
