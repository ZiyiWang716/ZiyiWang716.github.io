Fridgey

Brief

Fridgey is a virtual fridge that reminds users when their groceries go bad while providing other food management tools.

Category

Web App Development

Team Project

Role

UX Design, Front-end Development, Back-end Devlopment

Tool

Figma, React, Express, MongoDB

Product Vision

For the everyday grocery shopper, who needs help keeping track of items in their fridge, Fridgey is a mobile/web application simplifies fridge management and shopping planning.

Reflection & Future Step

For this project, our team followed the Agile principle for the whole development process and it worked out pretty well even all the team members were working remotely. Each one of us in the team worked as a full-stack developer through the whole process.On the other side, we did encounter many challenges during implementation but we had worked out most of them by helping each other when the person that was responsible for a specific function could not solve it in time.

Because of the time limit, our project did not implement the Photo Scanning and Settings. And our user login remained supporting only one user at a time with our current back-end code. These things would be our future steps if we are going to further develop Fridgey beyond an MVP.
